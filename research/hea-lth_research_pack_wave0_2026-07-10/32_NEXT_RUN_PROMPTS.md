## Universal continuation instructions

Apply these instructions to every wave below:

1. Read `00_README.md`, `02_SOURCE_LEDGER.csv`, `31_CONTINUATION_LEDGER.json`, `33_RESEARCH_PLAN_AND_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md`, and the wave-specific source-of-truth files before researching.
2. Do not redo Wave 0. Expand or correct it with dated evidence.
3. Preserve every existing source ID, risk ID, column name, evidence label, Hebrew keyword, URL state, and workstream number. Add rows and versions; do not silently overwrite provenance.
4. Research in Hebrew and English. Preserve Hebrew queries and titles exactly.
5. Prefer current Israeli primary sources, regulators, official registries, official product/policy/pricing/help/developer pages, clinical guidelines, systematic reviews, standards, and audited owner data.
6. Label every material statement as VERIFIED FACT, PUBLIC CLAIM, OWNER DATA, ESTIMATE, INFERENCE, HYPOTHESIS, RECOMMENDATION, or DATA REQUIRED.
7. For every important number record value, currency/unit, geography, period, source URL, source/publication date, access date, method, confidence, and limitations.
8. Do not invent search volume, CPC, keyword difficulty, rank, traffic, conversion, market size, price, provider count, license/status, product approval, revenue, or clinical outcomes.
9. Treat medicine, drugs, devices, diagnostics, aesthetics, surgery, fertility, longevity, health data, and patient-specific AI as high risk.
10. Do not recommend a general-purpose LLM for diagnosis, triage, treatment selection, medication advice, image analysis, or patient-specific clinical guidance.
11. Do not delete, redirect, merge, rename, or deindex a current URL without the complete legacy-URL evidence gate.
12. Preserve original URLs and update `02_SOURCE_LEDGER.csv` with every source used.
13. Update `28_RISK_REGISTER.csv`, `30_OPEN_QUESTIONS_AND_DATA_REQUESTS.md`, `31_CONTINUATION_LEDGER.json`, and `35_QA_SELF_AUDIT.md` at the end of the wave.
14. State what remains unverified and what requires a qualified Israeli lawyer, privacy adviser, clinician, pharmacist, medical-device expert, security professional, accessibility expert, or accountant.
15. When the wave is complete, do not claim the entire 36-workstream program is complete.
