# Medical Guardrails

Updated: 2026-05-27

## Operating Boundary

Hea-lth.co.il can coordinate private services, but it must not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or tell users whether a medical situation is safe to wait.

## Allowed Content

- Service explainers: what a second opinion is, how appointment coordination works, what documents a provider may request later.
- Payment explainers: private payment, SHABAN, private insurance, reimbursement process.
- Provider discovery: specialty, location, availability, and verified credentials.
- Reviewed educational content with named reviewer, sources, and update date.

## Disallowed Without Medical Review

- Diagnosis guidance.
- Treatment recommendations.
- Medication dosage or interactions.
- Emergency triage.
- Claims that a service is better, faster, guaranteed, risk-free, or medically suitable for a specific person.
- Automated health advice emails or chatbot replies.

## Emergency Copy

Every high-intent page should include:

> במקרה חירום רפואי, כאב חזה, קוצר נשימה, סימני שבץ, דימום חמור או סכנת חיים יש לפנות מיד למד״א 101 או לחדר מיון.

## Review Checklist Before Publishing

- Medical reviewer assigned.
- Sources are official or clinically reliable.
- No outcome guarantees.
- No hidden advertorial claims.
- Clear author/reviewer/date.
- Clear privacy and consent treatment for lead forms.
