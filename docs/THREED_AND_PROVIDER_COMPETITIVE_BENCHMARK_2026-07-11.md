## Benchmark decision

Hea-lth should not copy a generic anatomy viewer or a generic paid-provider directory. The distinctive product must be the governed connection between body, evidence-led choice, verified local supply, map location, and consent-aware lead operations. That connection is implemented as source contracts, but it is not yet a live advantage.
