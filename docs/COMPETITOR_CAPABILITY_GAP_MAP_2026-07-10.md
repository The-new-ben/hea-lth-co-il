# Global and Israeli Competitor Capability Gap Map

Date: 2026-07-10  
Method: official product/about/help pages plus localized Israeli SERP sampling

## Benchmark matrix

| Platform | Capability it owns | What Hea-lth should learn | Gap Hea-lth can target |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mayo Clinic | Disease/condition A-Z, symptom checker, clinical trials, appointments, patient account, professional resources | Organize medical information around user tasks and connect education to care | Not an independent Israeli marketplace; no Hebrew-local price/provider/device comparison layer |
| Cleveland Clinic | 6,000+ reviewed health-library articles across body systems, conditions, treatments, procedures, devices, tests, symptoms | Named expert review, source disclosure, scheduled re-review, strong taxonomy | Institution-centered, not a neutral multi-provider/commerce platform |
| WebMD | Broad encyclopedia, symptoms, drugs, tools, consumer portal | Breadth, interactive tools, lay-language navigation | Not localized to Israel; advertising-heavy model and no integrated Israeli provider/device transaction layer |
| Zocdoc | Guided provider search, verified licensing, insurance/location/availability filters, real-time booking, verified reviews, provider tools, EHR integrations | Patient-directed ranking, transparent search method, real availability, one-time new-booking model | Less encyclopedic/aesthetic/device-commerce depth and no Hebrew/Israeli payer context |
| Doctolib | Patient health companion, accounts, messaging, telehealth, intake, prevention, practitioner operations, safety-focused AI | A continuous account journey tied to professionals and local systems | No Israel localization; not a broad premium-treatment/device marketplace |
| RealSelf | Aesthetic treatments, vetted doctors, verified reviews, before/after media, doctor Q&A, consultation requests | Aesthetic proof, community questions, media standards, repeat license verification | Narrow to aesthetics, not full medicine/devices; no Israel-specific commerce and care navigation |
| MedReviews | Israeli provider directory, specialties, regions, languages, payer filters, reviews, contact | Strong local provider inventory and long-tail specialty/location SERPs | Limited encyclopedia, products, AI, 3D, and full transaction/account ecosystem |
| Estheticare | Israeli aesthetic/plastic information, price comparison, lead generation | Commercial page clarity and price-led acquisition | Weaker provider verification, evidence governance, account product, 3D, and broad medical authority |
| Henry Schein | Professional-only catalog, 190,000 products, history/reordering, credit, leasing, equipment, pharmaceuticals, practice services | Separate B2B permissions, catalog depth, quick ordering, credit/leasing, supplier operations | Not a consumer treatment/provider knowledge journey and not Israel-specific |
| DOTmed | New/used medical-equipment marketplace, verified vendors, auctions, RFPs, wanted listings, price data, featured listings | B2B marketplace, quote/RFP flow, vendor reputation, equipment lifecycle | Limited consumer medicine/editorial/booking and local clinical context |
| Crisalix | Patient-specific 3D/VR/AR aesthetic simulation and surgeon directory | Simulation as a consultation tool, not decorative 3D | Single vertical; Hea-lth can connect 3D to evidence, providers, booking, and broader device education |
| Prenuvo | Premium imaging membership, AI-enhanced analysis, biomarker panels, longitudinal dashboard, clinical team | Premium packaging, dashboard, baseline-to-repeat journey | Controversial evidence boundaries and single-service model; Hea-lth can compare options and limitations neutrally |
| Function Health | Large lab panels, membership, longitudinal results, scheduling, premium prevention | Membership economics and longitudinal visualization | Testing-focused, non-Israeli, and not a general marketplace |
| Infermedica / Ada | Structured symptom assessment and triage through clinically governed products/APIs | If triage is ever offered, use a validated specialist vendor and explicit non-diagnostic scope | Hea-lth should not reproduce this with a general LLM; opportunity starts with safe navigation and administrative AI |

## White-space hypothesis

No reviewed competitor in this sample combines all of the following in Hebrew for Israel:

1. encyclopedia-scale medically reviewed knowledge;
2. an entity-driven medical glossary and semantic search;
3. verified providers with transparent search and booking;
4. premium aesthetic/private-health comparison and concierge;
5. B2C and B2B medical-device commerce;
6. patient, professional, supplier, reviewer, and administrator accounts;
7. evidence-grounded AI plus governed 3D/AR;
8. one SEO architecture that connects education to decisions and transactions.

The largest gap is operational, not visual: verified supply, medical review, privacy, product regulation, booking data, and transparent commercial contracts must exist behind the interface.
