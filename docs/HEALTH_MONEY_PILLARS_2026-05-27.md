Date: 2026-05-27

Source mix: fresh web research, live SERP/category review, and Lovable research. Lovable completed the hea-lth.co.il run with `Used 27 tools`.

## Lovable Findings Captured

- Current hea-lth.co.il footprint is mostly informational, with very low commercial presence.
- Lovable reported an observed baseline of about `94` terms and roughly `15` monthly visits.
- Strong competitor pattern in aesthetics: Estheticare, reported at about `4,415` visits and `$3,196` traffic value.
- Strong marketplace/treatment-price pattern: `היועצת`, reported as a lean site with a treatment-price structure.
- Priority commercial pillars: כירורגיה פלסטית, רפואה אסתטית, השתלת שיער, לייזר, רפואה פרטית ודימות.

## Price Research Notes

Botox public ranges observed in fresh research:

- About `700` NIS for one area in one published clinic price example.
- About `1,500` NIS for three areas in one published clinic price example.
- About `2,500` to `3,500` NIS for broader face treatment in competitor/price-guide examples.

Rhinoplasty public ranges observed in fresh research:

- About `6,000` to `21,000` NIS in one marketplace-style competitor example.
- About `12,000` to `25,000` NIS for aesthetic rhinoplasty and `6,000` to `15,000` NIS for medical rhinoplasty in one surgeon/clinic example.
- About `12,000` to `40,000` NIS for combined or more complex cases in one surgeon/clinic example.

These are public reference ranges only. Public pages must state that final price is set after professional examination and clinic quote.

## CMS and Doctor Index Fields

Public fields:

- Doctor or clinic name.
- Specialty and sub-specialty.
- License or credential source where available.
- City and clinic area.
- Languages.
- Services/treatments offered.
- Consultation type: in-clinic, online, second opinion, imaging review.
- Price range or "price after consultation" where a public number is not responsible.
- Insurance/refund path: private insurance, Shaban, self-pay.
- Availability notes.
- Safety notes and emergency boundary.
- Date verified and reviewer/source.

Internal operations fields:

- Provider contact owner.
- Commercial status.
- Follow-up cadence.
- Lead handling path.
- Payment/invoice path.

Do not expose internal fields on public pages.

## Public Copy Rules

- Consumer-facing only.
- No CRM, leads, supplier routing, money-page, revenue, draft, or operating language.
- No diagnosis, treatment decision, guarantee, promised result, or emergency triage.
- Every medical/high-intent page needs emergency copy and a no-medical-advice boundary.
- Before/after images require consent, context, no promise, and no misleading editing.

## Premium Homepage Direction

Follow competitor DNA without copying:

- Estheticare-style: treatment category clarity, clean premium aesthetic visuals, direct price/consultation intent.
- Marketplace style from `היועצת`: simple treatment-price page structure, fast path to compare and act.
- Hea-lth should feel like a private health concierge plus medical-aesthetics marketplace, not an encyclopedia.

Visual direction:

- Deep teal, white, champagne/warm neutral accents.
- Premium clinic photography, not generic stock.
- Doctor/clinic trust blocks above the fold.
- Treatment cards with price-intent anchors.
- Short forms that ask for service, region, timeline, and contact details only.

## Critical Gap

The current live upgrade improves homepage visibility and publishes first-wave pillars, but the site is not yet competitive with the strongest aesthetics players. The next competitive jump requires real doctor/clinic index data, stronger logo, more generated/owned imagery, price comparison pages, schema, review/source governance, and better conversion forms.
