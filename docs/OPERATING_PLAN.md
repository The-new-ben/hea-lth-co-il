# hea-lth.co.il Operating Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-26

## Mission
Build a Hebrew health asset that can monetize safely. Health is YMYL, so trust, review, disclaimers, expert sourcing, and conservative claims are mandatory.

## Niche Hypothesis
Do not start with broad medical advice. Prioritize safer commercial and directory-style opportunities: private clinics, appointment leads, insurance/service comparisons, wellness services, dental/aesthetic/physiotherapy/mental-health provider discovery, and expert-reviewed explainers.

## Front Page Direction
Hebrew RTL health-service hub:
- find a clinic/service/provider
- condition/service categories
- appointment or consultation lead form
- strong medical disclaimers
- author/reviewer trust signals
- no exaggerated health claims

## Theme Decision
Use a custom lightweight WordPress theme synced through UPress/GitHub. Elementor can remain for noncritical pages, but core templates must support structured data, reviewer boxes, disclaimers, fast loading, and controlled internal linking.

## CRM/Funnel
CRM captures:
- service/condition category
- location
- urgency
- insurance/private preference
- consent and privacy status
- source page/keyword
Do not send medical advice automatically; responses should route users toward professional consultation and compliant lead handling.
